CheckOnce.js

Tab-tolerant notification subscription. Only polls for notifications from one tab, but seamlessly continues in another when the first one is closed.

Demo

Open up multiple tabs with this page and check their console. After you close the first tab, you'll notice that another takes up the notification-checking.


    var Notifier = new CheckOnce({
      checker: function(){
        console.log('Checking..');
        Notifier.emit({hello: 'world'});
      }
    });
